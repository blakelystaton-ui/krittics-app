Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Krittics ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ad-supported video on demand (AVOD) platform and services (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.
  • Profile Information: You may provide additional information such as profile pictures, interests, and preferences.
  • Communications: When you contact us, we collect the information you provide in your communications.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with our Service, including videos watched, viewing duration, trivia responses, game participation, and navigation patterns.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our Service, including device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Information: We may collect general location information based on your IP address.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers: When you sign in through third-party authentication services via Replit Auth, we receive basic profile information.
  • Analytics Providers: We receive aggregated analytics data from Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Personalize your experience and provide content recommendations
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity
  • Generate and display targeted advertising through Google AdSense and Ad Manager
  • Fulfill our obligations under applicable law

3. Third-Party Services We Use

Our Service integrates with the following third-party services:

3.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze usage of our Service. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit our Service, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our Service. For more information about Google Analytics' data practices, visit https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.

3.2 Google AdSense and Ad Manager

We use Google AdSense and Google Ad Manager to serve advertisements on our Service. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Service and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3.3 Firebase

We use Firebase for authentication, real-time database functionality, and backend operations. Firebase is provided by Google LLC. For more information about Firebase's data practices, visit https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

3.4 Google Gemini API

We use Google's Gemini API to generate interactive trivia content. The API processes movie-related data to create engaging quiz questions. For information about Google's AI data practices, visit https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3.5 Replit Hosting

Our Service is hosted on Replit's infrastructure. Replit may collect certain technical information necessary to provide hosting services. For more information, visit Replit's Privacy Policy.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, advertising, and customer support.
  • Advertising Partners: We share information with Google AdSense and Ad Manager to deliver targeted advertisements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: We may share or transfer your information in connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with your consent or at your direction.

IMPORTANT: We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

Krittics does not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with advertising partners for targeted advertising purposes, which may be considered "sharing" under certain privacy laws.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 General Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing of your information
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to our processing of your information

5.2 California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected, the sources of that information, our purposes for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: You have the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. While we do not sell personal information for monetary consideration, we may share information with advertising partners.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: You have the right to limit our use of sensitive personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

California "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"

  • Click "Your Privacy Choices" or "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" in our website footer

5.3 Nevada Privacy Rights

Nevada residents have the right to opt out of the sale of certain personal information. We do not sell personal information as defined under Nevada law.

5.4 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the rights listed in Section 5.1.
