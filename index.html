Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about Krittics

Q: What is the Krittics AVOD Model?

A: Krittics operates as an Advertising-Video-On-Demand (AVOD) platform. Our primary focus is on delivering a wide range of film and television content supported entirely by advertisements. This model allows us to offer all content to our users for free, while generating the revenue needed to sustain the platform and, critically, share profit with our community.

Q: How do users benefit from watching ads on Krittics?

A: Our unique value proposition is directly related to user engagement. As you watch advertisements on Krittics, you earn a fractional percentage of the resulting ad revenue. This creates a direct incentive for users to return and engage with our content, benefiting both the user and the Krittics ecosystem. This mechanism is designed to maximize **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)**, which is projected to be around **$1.05** based on our 200,000 MAU model.

Q: What is the target for monthly active users (MAUs) for Krittics?

A: Our primary operational target is **20,000 Monthly Active Users (MAUs)**. This target is set to comfortably exceed the break-even point and achieve a personal profit goal, which requires a minimum of 19,408 MAUs. Hitting 200,000 MAUs is our larger growth goal, which is projected to yield a substantial monthly net profit of **$166,600** and a healthy **79.3% profit margin**.

Q: What is Krittics' technology stack?

A: Krittics is built on a scalable and cost-effective technology stack. The frontend uses **Video.js** with a **Custom Video.js Plugin** for our video player interface. The backend is built using **Firebase Firestore/Realtime DB** for data, **Firebase Authentication**, and **Firebase Cloud Functions (Node.js)** for server-side logic and API calls, including the **Gemini API** for AI functionality. We use **Cloudflare Stream/R2** or **YouTube** for video delivery to keep egress costs low, aligning with our free/low-cost service strategy.

Q: How is Krittics monetized?

A: We rely on **Google Ad Manager / AdMob** for monetization. Our revenue stream is purely ad-supported (AVOD). Our 200,000 MAU model shows a total monthly ad revenue of **$210,000**, which covers fixed costs (like content costs) and variable expenses (like infrastructure costs of **$21,500**) and allows for the user payout of **$12,600**.

Q: Do you plan to add in-app purchases or subscriptions?

A: Currently, Krittics is focused on the free AVOD model with ad-revenue sharing. We have plans to potentially explore **in-app purchases** at a later date, but this is a future development priority.
